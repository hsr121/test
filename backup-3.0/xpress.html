From May 2021 until IDO of the XPRESS token in November of 2021, 3 additional rounds of XPRESS token private sales will occur, which will allow CryptoXpress to fund its development, operations, marketing and other business activities

November 2021
Stage 3
Public Trading/IDO

Upon the XPRESS launch, the tokens will be made available for public trading via the CryptoXpress app and other secondary markets, including on DEXs and CEXs.

The XPRESS token is a utility token for the CryptoXpress platform which will be used to provide discounts, rewards, and bonus functionality to investors. Transactions paid for using XPRESS tokens will be significantly discounted with the token also being a central component of the CryptoXpress retail partnership initiative which will promote retail payments through XPRESS tokens in exchange for various customer benefits.
Technology
BEP-20 Token
XPRESS runs on the BEP-20 network and is compiled with the latest version of Solidity, in order to avoid compiler bugs, along with a community audited contract. XPRESS can be stored on and transferred to and from most major cryptocurrency wallets including Meta Mask wallet.

XPRESS Token Value & Buyback Plan
